HR Analytics - Employees Engagement¶

In this EDA project we would be performing HR Analytics - Employees Engagement Analysis which is present in kaggle platform. In this EDA project we will analyse and visualize our dataset.

The job role of a HR is not that easy as it seems from the outside

The HR's have to actively participate in recruitment process, helping employees with their issues, maintaining positive environment, analysing the performance and efficiency, and many more.

Amonge all the job responsiblities of an HR, evaluating the performance and efficiency of the employees is considered the most difficult task.

The difficulty level of the task is directly proportional to number of employees who work under that particular HR.

Thus, to deal with it we have to come up with Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project.

Here we will be performing different analysis and visualizations using the Employees Engagement Dataset to obtain some valuable insights.
